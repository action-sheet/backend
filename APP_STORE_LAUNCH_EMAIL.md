## Apple App Store Launch Process

### Review & Approval Process
Apple employs a **manual review process** where an actual Apple employee will:
- Download and test our application on a real device
- Verify compliance with App Store guidelines
- Check for functionality, security, and user experience standards
- Review all app metadata, screenshots, and descriptions
- Test in-app features and workflows

This review typically takes **24-48 hours** but can extend to 5-7 days during peak periods.

### Apple Developer Program Requirements
To publish on the App Store, we need:

1. **Apple Developer Program Membership**
   - Cost: **$99 USD per year** (recurring annual subscription)
   - This is a mandatory requirement to distribute apps on the App Store
   - Includes access to development tools, beta testing (TestFlight), and app analytics

2. **Company Information Required**
   - Legal business name: Al-Ahlia Contracting Group (W.L.L.)
   - D-U-N-S Number (if enrolling as organization)
   - Business verification documents

3. **App Submission Requirements**
   - App binary (compiled application)
   - App icon and screenshots (various iPhone/iPad sizes)
   - Privacy policy URL
   - App description and keywords
   - Support contact information

### Ongoing Costs
- **Annual renewal**: $99 USD/year to keep the app published
- If subscription lapses, the app will be removed from the App Store

## Google Play Store Launch Process

### Review & Approval Process
Google uses a **combination of automated and manual review**:
- Initial automated security and policy scans (1-2 hours)
- Manual review by Google team if flagged (24-48 hours)
- Generally faster than Apple's process
- Less strict guidelines compared to Apple

### Google Play Console Requirements
To publish on Google Play, we need:

1. **Google Play Developer Account**
   - **One-time registration fee**: $25 USD (lifetime access)
   - No annual renewal required
   - Much more affordable than Apple

2. **Company Information Required**
   - Developer name: Al-Ahlia Contracting Group
   - Contact email and phone number
   - Privacy policy URL

3. **App Submission Requirements**
   - Android App Bundle (AAB file)
   - App icon and feature graphic
   - Screenshots (phone and tablet)
   - App description and category
   - Content rating questionnaire

### Ongoing Costs
- **No annual fees** - the $25 registration is lifetime
- Optional: Google Play Console advanced features (analytics, A/B testing)

## Additional Considerations

### App Store Optimization (ASO)
- App name, description, and keywords impact discoverability
- Screenshots and preview videos increase download rates
- Regular updates improve app store ranking

### Post-Launch Requirements
- **Apple**: Must respond to user reviews and maintain app updates
- **Google**: Similar requirements, plus compliance with new Android versions
- Both platforms require privacy policy updates if data handling changes

### Enterprise Distribution (Alternative)
If the app is only for internal company use, we could consider:
- **Apple Business Manager** (enterprise distribution, no App Store needed)
- **Google Play Private Apps** (internal distribution only)
- These options avoid public app store requirements but have different setup processes
